Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my monthly donation?

Yes, you can pause, change, or cancel your recurring donation at any time through your donor dashboard.

How is my donation used?

Donations are allocated to our most urgent verified clusters unless you specify a particular cause (e.g., Education, Water, Healthcare).

Do you accept other payment methods?

Yes, we accept bank transfers, PayPal, and crypto donations. Please contact finance@jeevan.org for details.
