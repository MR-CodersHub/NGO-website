Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donations, volunteering, and our programs.

üí∞ Donations

How can I make a donation?

You can donate through our website using credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, or digital wallets. Simply visit our Donate page, choose a cause or make a general donation, enter your preferred amount, and complete the secure payment process. You can also set up recurring monthly donations.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Jeevan Foundation is a registered 80G organization. All donations are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive a tax receipt via email within 24-48 hours of your donation that you can use for tax purposes.

Can I donate in honor of someone?

Absolutely! During the donation process, you can dedicate your gift in honor or memory of someone special. We can also send a personalized notification card to the honoree or their family letting them know about your generous gift.

How is my donation used?

We ensure maximum impact by allocating 85% of donations directly to programs. The remaining 15% covers administrative and fundraising costs. We publish annual reports and impact updates so you can see exactly how your contribution makes a difference.

ü§ù Volunteering

How can I become a volunteer?

Getting started is easy! Create an account on our website, fill out the volunteer application form, and select your areas of interest. Our volunteer coordinator will contact you within 5 business days to discuss opportunities that match your skills and availability.

What is the minimum time commitment?

We value every minute you can spare! We have opportunities ranging from one-time events (3-4 hours) to ongoing commitments (4-8 hours per month). You choose what works best for your schedule, and we'll find the right fit.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We have many virtual volunteering opportunities including content writing, social media management, graphic design, translation services, online tutoring, and administrative support. These allow you to contribute from anywhere in the world.

üìö Programs

What programs does Jeevan Foundation run?

We focus on four key areas: Education (scholarships, school supplies, teacher training), Healthcare (mobile clinics, health camps), Clean Water (well construction, water purification), and Livelihood (skill training, microfinance). Each program is designed for sustainable, long-term impact.

Where are your programs located?

Our programs operate across 42 countries, with major presence in India, Africa, and Southeast Asia. We work primarily in underserved rural and urban communities where our intervention can create maximum impact.

üë§ Account

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" link on our website and fill in your details. You can also sign up using your Google account for faster registration. Having an account allows you to track donations, volunteer hours, and receive personalized updates.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or contact us at support@jeevan.org.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help. Reach out to us and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
