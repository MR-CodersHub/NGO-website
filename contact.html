Support & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for our partners and volunteers.

How do you select your regional project hubs?

We conduct deep-level socio-economic audits and engage with indigenous community leaders for 6-12 months before establishing a permanent coordinate hub.

What is the Handover Protocol for completed projects?

Once a community achieves financial and technical autonomy, we exit operational control, remaining solely as an advisory partner with quarterly oversight.

Can institutional partners direct capital to specific clusters?

Yes. While our general fund handles urgent relief, partners can specify clusters like Solar Water, Mobile Health, or Vocational Digital Training.
