After receiving training and capital from Jeevan Foundation, Anjali's village established a self-sustaining cooperative that now employs 120 families. This project reduced local unemployment by 65% in just 18 months.

The Jeevan Method

How We Create Systemic Change

01

Deep Assessment

We spend 6 months in a region before spending a single dollar, mapping local bottlenecks and existing community assets.

02

Council Assembly

We facilitate the election of local leaders who take 100% ownership of program design and execution strategies.

03

Exit & Sustain

Our goal is to leave. We build revenue models for every project so they remain solvent without perpetual external aid.
